About Me

I am Mehedi Hasan Mamun, a student at Patuakhali Science and Technology University (PSTU) in the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department. I graduated in 2020 from Cantonment Public School and College, Bir Uttam Shaheed, as a Science group student.

I am a volunteer with the "Raktodaner Opekkhay Bangladesh" group, which focuses on blood donation awareness. I enjoy working with technology, particularly web development and Arduino projects.

My goal is to bring about positive change in society through technology and innovation. I aim to make my mother proud and honor her through my achievements.

Goal: To bring efficient and effective solutions to people's lives through technology in the future.
